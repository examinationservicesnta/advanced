Step 1: Fill JEE (Advanced) 2025 Application Form

Login with JEE (Main) 2025 Application number and JEE (Main) 2025 Password. On successful login, all personal, academic details as furnished to JEE (Main) 2025 will be displayed only for the candidates who have qualified for JEE (Advanced) 2025. Candidates are required to furnish additional information for JEE (Advanced) 2025.

Step 2: Upload Scanned Certificates

Candidates are required to upload the following scanned certificates:

S.No. Certificate to be uploaded Remarks Format Min Size (KB)
